# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and professional in all project spaces.

## Expected behavior

- Be polite and collaborative.
- Give feedback focused on code and ideas, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification when needed.
- Respect different backgrounds, identities, and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting, or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Repeated bad-faith interactions after warning.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments/issues/PRs, and may limit participation for violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report to maintainers via GitHub issue or direct contact.
